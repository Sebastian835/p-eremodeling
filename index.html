Who We Are

Built on Experience,
Driven by Excellence

P&E Remodeling & Construction LLC is a professional company with over 15 years of hands-on experience in residential and light commercial projects. We specialize in delivering high-quality craftsmanship, reliable service, and complete solutions.

From small repairs to full renovations, we handle every project with the same level of commitment and professionalism — always focusing on durable finishes and functional design.

  Our Mission

To provide high-quality remodeling and construction services, delivering safe, functional, and visually appealing spaces while maintaining honesty, professionalism, and customer satisfaction in every project.

  Our Vision

To become a trusted and recognized remodeling and construction company in Illinois, known for quality workmanship, reliability, and long-term client relationships.
